The classic dart. (easy)

  1. Get a rectangular sheet of paper
  2. Take the longer edge and fold it to the side like in the picture.
  3. Open
  4. Get the right side and fold to the center.
  5. Repeat on the other side
  6. Fold the right side again
  7. Repeat on the other side.
  8. Fold in half.
  9. Fold from the nose until the top edge hits the bottom and repeat on the other side and done.
