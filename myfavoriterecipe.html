Red sauce pasta(vegetarian)

    ingredients

  • 1/4 cup good quality extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 medium white or yellow onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed or diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt, or kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil, chiffonade

    Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Add in the canned tomatoes, with juices, and season well with salt and pepper. Add a pinch of sugar. Allow the sauce to simmer for 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Remove the pan from heat and stir in the basil just before serving. Serve with your choice of pasta or other dishes.
